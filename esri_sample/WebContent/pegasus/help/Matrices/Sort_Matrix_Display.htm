Sorting a Matrix Display

You can sort the matrix display rows for any of the five Pegasus matrices - Network, Schedule Overlay, Schedule, Volume Utilization, and Allocation.

To (optionally) sort by a column or columns, simply click in the column heading:

  • First, displays and the rows are sorted according to ascending values in the column.

  • Or, click again to display and sort in descending order.

  • Or, click again to clear the sort.

Note: When you sort by multiple columns, the sort/sub-sort order follows the order in which you activate the sorts.

Viewing lane and leg detail

The map and schematic contain a lot more information than simply how lanes or legs connect between locations. On maps you can hover over lanes to see their endpoints and direction, you can hover over legs to see their endpoints and status. On both Maps and Schematics you can click on lines to get much more detail about what the line represents.

To view lane or leg detail:

  1. Hover over a line until it highlights. You may need to zoom in to make it easier to select a specific line.

  2. Click the line. The Detail pop-up opens. You may need to increase the size of the map or schematic window to see the entire pop-up:

To change the content of the Detail pop-up, do any of the following:

View volume data for a different lane or leg

To see volume data for a different lane or leg, click next to the lane or leg of interest.  

 

View volume data for a different day

  1. In the View volumes for day box, type one or more days, for example 1-5,7,10. Or, to use the calendar, click . You see the following:

    • a calendar showing Plan days. To change the calendar to Month days, click

    • the currently displayed day highlighted in blue

    • the operating days for the lanes or legs on the map highlighted in white

To select different operating days from the calendar:

    • to select a single day, click a day

    • to select all the days in a week, click a week on the left edge of the calendar

    • to select all the same days in a month, click a day column header at the top of the calendar

Turn on or off display of Weight, Cube, or Piece-related columns

  1. Open the settings drawer(Click on the right side of the window).

  2. Check or uncheck boxes in the Lane Detail - Show columns on matrix section.

  3. Click Apply.

 

Change the products displayed

The Volume Groups settings in the Application Settings determines which products are displayed in the Lane and Leg Detail. You can display products from only one product group at a time.

To select a different Volume Group:

    • By default, all products in a group are displayed. To turn off the display of any product, clear the product checkbox.

  3. Click Save.

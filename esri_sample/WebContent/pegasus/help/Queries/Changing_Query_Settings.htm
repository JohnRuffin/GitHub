Changing Query Settings

From the Query Settings drawer, you can change settings on the Network and Schedule tab to hide fields or subwindows that you typically wonâ€™t use. On the Network tab, you can also set at what level, Full Routing or Lane Level, the map displays results.

You can reset the settings at any time if you decide later that you want to enable settings you had previously hidden.  You can also create a favorite so that you can easily switch between different customizations. For more information, see Saving and Managing Query Favorites and Use a query favorite.

  1. To open the Query Settings drawer, click the drawer handle.

     

The options you see depend on which tab you are in (Network or Schedule) when you open the drawer.

On the Network Query Settings:

    • To show the map results using Full Routing or Lane Level routing, click the appropriate radio button.

    • To show or hide fields in the General, Previous, Primary, or Next subwindows, click the checkbox next to the field name to show (checked) or hide (unchecked) the field.

On the Schedule Query Settings:

    • To show the map results by Route or by Leg, click the appropriate radio button. (Note: The by Leg option is not available in this release).

    • To show or hide legs that use a particular mode, click the checkbox to display (checked) or hide (unchecked) the mode.

    • To show or hide subwindows on the Schedule query tab, click the checkbox under Display to show (checked) or hide (unchecked) the subwindow.

    • To show a subwindow expanded or collapsed by default each time you open the Schedule tab, click the checkbox under Expand to expand (checked) or collapse (unchecked) the subwindow.

  1. To close the Query Settings drawer, click the drawer handle.
